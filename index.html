🎮 How to Play African Crostic

A Fun Way to Learn About African Culture!

🎯 Objective

Fill in the crossword grid with the correct letters based on the clues provided. When you complete the grid correctly, a mystery phrase will be revealed at the top of the screen!

📝 Step-by-Step Guide

1

Choose a Category

Select from topics like African Culture, History, Science, Music, or Entertainment.

2

Select a Puzzle

Each category has multiple puzzles with different difficulty levels. Pick one that interests you!

3

Read the Clues

On the right side, you'll see a list of clues. Each clue has a number that matches the grid cells and tells you how many letters are in the answer.

4

Fill in the Grid

Click on any cell in the grid and type your answer. The game will automatically move to the next cell.

  • ✅ Green cell = Correct answer
  • ❌ Red cell = Incorrect answer
  • 🔢 Each cell has a number that matches the clue numbers
5

Reveal the Mystery Phrase

As you fill in correct answers, letters will appear in the puzzle phrase at the top. Complete the entire grid to reveal the full mystery phrase!

⌨️ Keyboard Controls

A-Z Type letters to fill in cells
Backspace Delete current cell or go back
Arrow Keys Navigate between cells
Enter Move to next cell
Esc Go to previous cell

🎮 Game Controls

✅ Check Answer Verifies all your answers and highlights correct/incorrect cells
🔄 Reset Puzzle Clears the entire grid and starts fresh
➡️ Next Puzzle Loads the next puzzle in the current category
← Back Return to previous screen

💡 Pro Tips

  • 💬 Click on any clue to jump directly to its first cell in the grid
  • 🎵 Listen for sound effects that give audio feedback for your actions
  • 🧩 Start with easier puzzles and work your way up
  • 🌍 Learn about African culture as you play - each clue teaches you something new!
  • 🏆 Try to complete puzzles without using the Check Answer button for extra challenge
